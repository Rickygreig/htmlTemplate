Hello, and Welcome to My First Website!

The first webiste I have used a template to make!

My name is Ricky and as you can tell, this is the first website that I have made. I started my early career as an ICU Nurse. After about 5 years in the ICU (a year on the front lines of COVID), I decided I can do better. My options for nursing were either go back to school for 2-4 more years, or go into hospital administation. Neither option sounded very apealing to me. This is when my friend Brooks Ryba introduced me to the world of programing!
